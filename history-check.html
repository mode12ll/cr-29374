What We Check

Our comprehensive vehicle history reports include:

Written-off Status

Check if the vehicle has previously been declared a write-off by an insurer.

Stolen Status

Verify that the vehicle is not currently recorded as stolen in police records.

Financial Encumbrance

Discover if the vehicle has outstanding finance owing that could become your responsibility.

Identify potential odometer tampering by analyzing the vehicle's recorded history.

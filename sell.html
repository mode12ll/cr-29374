Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to list my car?

We offer a free standard listing option that runs for 30 days. Premium and Ultimate packages are available for $49 and $89 respectively, offering additional features to help sell your car faster.

How long will my listing be active?

Standard listings are active for 30 days, Premium listings for 60 days, and Ultimate listings for 90 days. You can renew or upgrade your listing at any time.

How do buyers contact me?

Buyers can contact you through our secure messaging system, by email, or by phone depending on your preferences. Your contact information is only shared with genuine buyers who express interest in your vehicle.

Can I edit my listing after it's published?

Yes, you can edit your listing details, add or remove photos, and adjust the price at any time through your seller dashboard. Keeping your listing updated can help attract more potential buyers.

What happens after I sell my car?

Once you've sold your car, you can mark it as sold in your dashboard. We'll remove it from active search results and provide you with guidance on completing the sale safely, including transfer of ownership documents.
